Vibrand understands the importance of a holistic approach to market research. We therefore have a diverse set of offerings that ensure that our clients walk away with in-depth real world data, that can be applied to grow their businesses. Find out more about the specific services we offer below.

Qualitative

We offer all the usual qual solutions – from focus groups to immersions; from deep consumer work to B2B interviews.

Quantitative

We pride ourselves in providing bespoke Quantitative solutions, meaning we’ll never box you into a ‘model’: every study we design is based on its own merits and your individual needs.

Sensory Research

Understand the relationship between your product and your customers. We manage and interpret superb tasting panels.

Social Research

Social research is conducted to improve and expand on our knowledge of the world by providing decision makers in social policy and intervention projects with the best data possible.
