Having conducted a number of tasting panels for our clients, we have now set up a sister company to Vibrand specialising in this work. The Tasting Panel conducts, well, tasting panels. Well, The Tasting Panel conducts tasting panels well!

We take a relaxed approach to field, with in home taste testing – combined with the most up-to-date statistical analysis and software.
