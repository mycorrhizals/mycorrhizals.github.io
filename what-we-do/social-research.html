Social research is conducted to improve and expand on our knowledge of the world by providing decision makers in social policy and intervention projects with the best data possible.

We conduct social research in 54 countries through Reach, and run extensive analytical social media research projects through Opinion.

We now process all this work through our sister company, the Citizen Research Centre.
